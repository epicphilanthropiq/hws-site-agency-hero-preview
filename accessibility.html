Accessibility Statement

Accessibility is part of the platform's purpose and will continue to be improved as the website and products mature.

Last reviewed: 16 July 2026 · Draft for final legal review before public launch.
Important

This document explains the intended operating approach of Hello World Studio. It is not a substitute for advice from a qualified legal professional.

Our commitment

Hello World Studio aims to make its website, products and learning experiences usable by people with different abilities, devices and access conditions.

Current accessibility foundations

The website includes semantic headings, skip links, keyboard-accessible navigation, visible focus styles, responsive layouts, text alternatives, reduced-motion support and sufficiently large touch targets where implemented.

Voice accessibility roadmap

A zero-cost browser-based accessibility assistant is planned after the main site is complete. The intended features include read-aloud controls, supported voice dictation and limited spoken navigation commands. It will remain optional and will not replace screen-reader compatibility.

Known limitations

Some external checkout pages, embedded tools, browser speech features and third-party platforms are outside Hello World Studio's direct control. Voice-recognition support may vary by browser, device, accent, speech pattern and network connection.

Feedback

Accessibility feedback can be sent to helloworldstudiozw@gmail.com. Please include the page, device, browser and barrier encountered.

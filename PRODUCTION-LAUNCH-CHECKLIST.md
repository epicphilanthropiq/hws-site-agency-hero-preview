# Hello World Studio Production Launch Checklist

## Domain and indexing
- Confirm the final production domain.
- Replace all preview canonical and sitemap URLs.
- Remove page-level noindex only after approval.
- Remove the global X-Robots-Tag noindex header.
- Replace robots.txt with production crawling rules.
- Submit the final sitemap to search engines.

## Products and checkout
- Confirm every Beacons product URL.
- Confirm displayed prices and currencies.
- Test every Buy, Launch, Documentation and Support button.
- Confirm licence and delivery instructions.

## Client proof
- Obtain written permission for every client name, visual and testimonial.
- Add only verified results.
- Remove pending placeholders that are not approved for public launch.

## Legal and privacy
- Obtain qualified legal review of policies.
- Confirm refund rules match Beacons settings and local obligations.
- Confirm business contact details.
- Confirm copyright and trademark usage.

## Accessibility
- Keyboard-test all pages.
- Screen-reader test key journeys.
- Verify contrast and focus states.
- Check alt text and form labels.
- Add the optional voice accessibility layer after final site completion.

## Technical
- Test mobile, tablet and desktop.
- Check 404 routes and redirects.
- Compress final images.
- Test forms and mailto drafts.
- Review security headers.
- Run performance and accessibility audits.

## Analytics
- Add analytics only after choosing a privacy approach.
- Avoid loading unnecessary trackers.
- Document any cookies or third-party tracking.

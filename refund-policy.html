Digital Product & Refund Policy

This draft explains the intended approach to digital delivery, technical support and refund requests.

Last reviewed: 16 July 2026 · Draft for final legal review before public launch.
Important

This document explains the intended operating approach of Hello World Studio. It is not a substitute for advice from a qualified legal professional.

Digital delivery

Digital products may be delivered through Beacons, a download link, a licence, a browser application or another clearly stated method.

Before purchasing

Buyers should review the product description, compatibility information, delivery method and current feature list before completing payment. Planned features are not part of the current purchase unless explicitly stated.

Refund requests

Because digital products can be accessed or copied immediately, refund eligibility may be limited after access has been delivered. A request should include the order details, reason and evidence of a technical or delivery problem.

Technical problems

Hello World Studio should be given a reasonable opportunity to provide the correct file, access instructions, troubleshooting or an alternative delivery method before a refund decision is made.

Done-for-you services

Custom services follow the written scope, payment schedule and cancellation terms agreed for that project. Deposits may cover reserved time and work already completed.

Contact

Send purchase or delivery questions to helloworldstudiozw@gmail.com with the product name and order information.

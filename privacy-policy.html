Privacy Policy

This policy explains the current privacy approach for the Hello World Studio website, digital products and client enquiries.

Last reviewed: 16 July 2026 · Draft for final legal review before public launch.
Important

This document explains the intended operating approach of Hello World Studio. It is not a substitute for advice from a qualified legal professional.

Information you choose to provide

When you email Hello World Studio, submit an enquiry through your own email application, purchase through Beacons, or contact us through another channel, you may provide your name, email address, organisation, project details and other information needed to respond.

How the guided contact page works

The website's guided contact form prepares an email draft on your device. The website does not operate a form database and does not send the message automatically. Your chosen email provider handles the final message.

Product data stored on your device

Some Hello World Studio web tools use browser storage to save projects locally. This information may disappear if browser data is cleared, private browsing is used, or the user changes devices. Export tools should be used where available.

Payments and checkout

Product payments are completed through the Hello World Studio Beacons Store or another clearly identified payment provider. Hello World Studio does not receive or store full payment-card details through this website.

AI providers and third parties

When a user copies information into an external AI platform, distributor, email provider or other third-party service, that service's privacy terms apply. Users should avoid submitting confidential or sensitive information unless they understand the provider's handling practices.

Client work and case studies

Client names, testimonials, project visuals and performance information will only be published with appropriate permission. Unverified claims are not intentionally presented as established results.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to helloworldstudiozw@gmail.com.

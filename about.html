Our story

Born from barriers.
Built around possibility.

Hello World Studio grew from lived experience: navigating disability, limited resources, creative ambition and the belief that technology can open doors when it is shaped around real human needs.

01

Creating a voice where barriers existed

The studio was shaped by the reality of cerebral palsy, speech barriers and the need to communicate ideas in ways that are clear, powerful and independent.

02

Turning AI into an accessibility tool

AI became more than a trend. It became a practical collaborator for writing, presentation, design, music, education and storytelling.

03

Building one connected ecosystem

Instead of creating disconnected services, Hello World Studio brings learning, content, products, music, publishing, business and legacy together.

Meet Shami

An AI presenter created to
expand access and expression.

Shami is the studio’s AI presenter, educator and creative interface. She helps communicate ideas clearly, host learning experiences and support storytelling where speech barriers or production limits might otherwise stand in the way.

She does not replace the founder’s voice. She extends its reach.

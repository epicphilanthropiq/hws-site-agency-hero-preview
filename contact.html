Common questions

Helpful answers before
you get in touch.

Can I contact you if I have a very small budget?

Yes. Be honest about the budget. We may recommend a digital product, a focused strategy session or a smaller first phase instead of a full service.

Do I need to understand AI?

No. The process begins with your goal. Technical explanations are kept practical and connected to what you are trying to do.

Can Hello World Studio work with clients outside Zimbabwe?

Yes. The studio is Zimbabwe-based and designed to work remotely with clients, creators and organisations across Africa and the diaspora.

Do you work with disabled founders and creators?

Yes. Accessibility is central to the studio’s mission. Share your communication or participation needs so the process can be adapted appropriately.

Can I combine several services?

Yes. The ecosystem is designed to connect. A legacy project may become a book, a song may need a release campaign, or an AI course may lead into a business system.

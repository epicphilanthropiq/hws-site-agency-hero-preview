Frequently asked questions

Clear answers before
you take the next step.

Is this a done-for-you service or software?

It is one product family with two editions. The Done-for-You Edition is delivered by Hello World Studio for one brand. The Agency Edition gives freelancers and agencies reusable browser-based access.

Does the Agency Edition require installation?

No. It runs in a modern web browser and stores the current workspace on the user's device.

Can I use it for several clients?

Yes. The Agency Edition includes a reusable client library for managing multiple saved briefs and content systems.

Does it create finished social posts automatically?

It creates structured platform-specific strategies and prompts. The user still reviews, edits and produces the final content.

Where is payment completed?

The Agency Edition is purchased through the Hello World Studio Beacons Store. Done-for-You work is quoted separately.

Frequently asked questions

Clear answers before
you take the next step.

Is HitMaker ZW a separate product?

No. HitMaker ZW is an embedded module inside HW Release System Studio.

Is this a music distribution service?

It is a release planning and preparation system. Distribution itself is completed through the artist's chosen distributor.

Who is the system designed for?

It is designed for independent artists, producers, small labels, worship teams and phone-first creators who do not have a large release team.

Does it replace human creative decisions?

No. The system organises the process, but the artist still chooses the sound, story, visuals, release timing and final campaign.

Can Hello World Music support the campaign?

Yes. The related support section connects artists to music, content and brand services inside Hello World Studio.
